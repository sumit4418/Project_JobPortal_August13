Job Description

Team handling experience must

Roles & Responsibilities:

Oversee and manage multiple teams of developers.

Provide technical oversight to development teams and help architect, design, and develop web application solutions.

Ensure SDLC, best practices, and coding standards are being followed.

Attract, recruit, and retain capable individuals to build high-performant teams.

Manage personnel actions, which includes but is not limited to, approving vacation, training, and making promotional salary or merit increase recommendations.

Maintain a culture of continuous improvement with a focus on being a best in class technology organization.

Maintain a culture of delivering on-time, on-scope, on-budget, and on-quality.

Other duties, as necessary.

Skills Required

Minimum of 10 years Microsoft SQL Server, TSQL, and relational database design experience.

Minimum of 10 years .NET web development experience (ASP.NET, C#, Web API).

At least 3-5 years of experience with Angular. Experience with other JavaScript frameworks is a plus.

8 years of leadership experience managing development teams at an IT/Software company.

Experience implementing and coaching agile teams to be more effective.

Experience implementing CI/CD.

Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

Self-motivated, results oriented, and strong sense of accountability.

Strong organizational and prioritization skills.

Passion for learning new technologies
